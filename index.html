Attorney Tamara Evelyn

Attorney Tamara Evelyn is a proud graduate of Mass. School of Law. She worked as an Assistant D.A. practicing in both Juvenile Court and District Court from 2014 to 2016. She established her solo practice in 2016 with a primary focus on family law, juvenile law and civil litigation.

She is a member of Mass Bar Association and Plymouth County Bar Association. In May of 2022, she was awarded the Emerging Defender Award for her commitment to indigent defense.
